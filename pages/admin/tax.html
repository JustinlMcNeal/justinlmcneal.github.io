📌 Notes

• Single-member LLC files Schedule C with Form 1040. No separate business return needed.

• Even if net profit ≈ $0, you must file to report business activity and protect against overpaying SE tax.

• Sales tax collected is a pass-through liability — not taxable income. Remit to your state separately.

• Home office deduction may apply if you use a dedicated space. Use Form 8829 or simplified method ($5/sq ft, max 300 sq ft).

• Product costs (COGS) shown here are estimated from supplier unit costs and shipping. Cross-reference with actual invoices.
