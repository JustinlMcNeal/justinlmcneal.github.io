Important Information

Shipping Address

Please double-check your shipping address at checkout. We cannot modify addresses once an order has been placed.

Tracking

All orders include tracking. You'll receive your tracking number via email once your order ships.

Business Days

Processing and delivery times are calculated in business days (Monday-Friday, excluding holidays).

US Shipping Only

We currently only ship within the United States. International shipping coming soon!

Possible Delays

During peak seasons (holidays, sales events), processing and shipping times may be slightly longer than usual. We appreciate your patience and will always keep you updated on your order status.
