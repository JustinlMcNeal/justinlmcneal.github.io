Data Deletion Request

At KarryKraze, we respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data. This page explains how you can request the deletion of any personal information we may have collected about you.

What Data We Collect

When you interact with our website or social media integrations, we may collect:

  • Email address (if you contact us or subscribe)
  • Name and contact information (if provided)
  • Order history and preferences
  • Social media interactions (likes, comments on our posts)

How to Request Data Deletion

To request deletion of your personal data, please contact us using one of the following methods:

Email: privacy@karrykraze.com

Subject Line: Data Deletion Request

Include: Your full name, email address associated with your account, and a description of the data you want deleted.

What Happens After Your Request

  • We will verify your identity to protect your privacy
  • We will process your request within 30 days
  • You will receive confirmation once your data has been deleted
  • Some data may be retained for legal or legitimate business purposes (e.g., order records for tax compliance)

Facebook/Instagram Data

If you've interacted with us through Facebook or Instagram, you can also manage your data directly through those platforms:

  • Facebook App Settings - Remove app permissions
  • Instagram Connected Apps - Manage connected apps

Questions?

If you have any questions about our data practices or this deletion process, please visit our Privacy Policy or Contact Us.
